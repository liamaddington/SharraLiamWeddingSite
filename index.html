Our Love Story

Unlike most online love stories, this couple met through an anonymous online chatting platform. They were officially introduced by a friend they met online under the pseudonym Nita.

Since then, they have never stopped updating and chatting with each other. Less than a month of knowing each other, they both decided to give dating a try.

After a few weeks, Liam decided to Sharra and booked his flight ticket to the Philippines. Unfortunately, on the same date of his scheduled arrival, on March 15, 2020, the Philippine borders were closed for all international and domestic flights due to the pandemic. They had to postpone their first meeting. While waiting for the borders to open for international flights, they consistently talked online and supported each others’ endeavours in life.

On February 23, 2022, Liam was finally able to meet Sharra and her family in the Philippines. This meeting made them both decide that they want to marry each other. Exactly a year later, on February 23, 2023, Sharra arrived in the UK.

They got married in Aylesbury, Buckinghamshire on June 15, 2023.
